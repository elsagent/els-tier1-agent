SALTO Electronic Lock - Door Not Locking Troubleshooting
=========================================================

COMMON CAUSES
A door that will not lock is usually caused by misalignment, a stuck latch
bolt, or the lock being set to passage mode (always unlocked).

STEP-BY-STEP TROUBLESHOOTING

Check Door Alignment
1. Close the door slowly and watch the latch bolt (the metal piece that
   extends from the door edge). It should slide smoothly into the strike
   plate on the door frame.

2. If the latch bolt does not line up with the strike plate hole, the door
   or frame may have shifted. Look for visible gaps or rubbing marks.

3. If misalignment is minor, the strike plate screws can be loosened and
   the plate repositioned. For major misalignment, contact maintenance.

Check the Latch Bolt
4. Manually push the latch bolt in with your finger and release it. It
   should spring back out smoothly.

5. If the latch bolt is stuck, stiff, or does not spring back, the lock
   mechanism may need cleaning or repair. Do not force it. Escalate to
   maintenance or Tier 2 support.

6. Check that nothing is blocking the latch bolt hole in the strike plate
   (debris, paint, or a bent plate).

Check for Passage Mode
7. Passage mode keeps the lock unlocked at all times without needing a
   card. This is common in conference rooms or during events.

8. To check if passage mode is active: present a staff card to the lock.
   If it flashes a specific pattern (often alternating green), passage
   mode may be on.

9. To disable passage mode, present an authorized staff card and follow
   the lock's toggle procedure: typically, present the card, then press
   and hold the interior thumb-turn or handle in the locked position
   for 3 seconds.

10. In ProAccess SPACE, you can also check and change the passage mode
    setting. Go to the door's properties and look for the Passage Mode
    or Office Mode toggle. Set it to OFF.

AFTER FIXING
11. Test the door by closing it and confirming the latch bolt engages.
12. Present a card and verify the lock and unlock cycle works normally.

If the door still does not lock after these steps, escalate to Tier 2
support or contact your locksmith.
