SALTO ProAccess SPACE - Password Reset Guide
=============================================

If you have forgotten your ProAccess SPACE password, follow these steps
to reset it. You will need access to the email address linked to your
ProAccess account.

STEP-BY-STEP PASSWORD RESET

1. Open ProAccess SPACE in your web browser. This is usually accessed at
   your property's server address (for example, https://your-hotel.salto.ws
   or a local network address).

2. On the login screen, click the "Forgot Password?" link below the
   password field.

3. Enter the email address associated with your ProAccess SPACE account.
   This is typically your work email that was used when your account was
   created.

4. Click "Send" or "Reset Password" to submit the request.

5. Check your email inbox for a password reset message from SALTO. This
   usually arrives within 1-5 minutes.

6. If you do not see the email, check your Spam or Junk folder.

7. Open the email and click the password reset link. This link is valid
   for a limited time (usually 24 hours).

8. On the reset page, enter a new password. SALTO passwords typically
   require at least 8 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase,
   and numbers.

9. Confirm the new password by typing it a second time.

10. Click "Save" or "Reset" to set your new password.

11. Go back to the ProAccess SPACE login page and sign in with your
    email and new password.

IF THE RESET EMAIL DOES NOT ARRIVE

12. Wait at least 10 minutes and check Spam/Junk again.

13. Confirm you are using the correct email address. Ask your manager
    or IT administrator which email is linked to your account.

14. Try the reset process one more time.

15. If the email still does not arrive, your account may not have an
    email on file, or the email server may be down. Contact your
    property's IT administrator or SALTO system administrator to
    manually reset your password.

IMPORTANT NOTES
- Do not share your password with other staff members. Each person
  should have their own account.
- If you suspect someone else has accessed your account, change your
  password immediately and notify your manager.
- Write down your new password and store it in a secure location.
