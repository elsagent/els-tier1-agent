SALTO Electronic Lock - Firmware Update Guide
===============================================

Firmware is the software that runs inside each SALTO lock. Updating it
can fix bugs, improve performance, and add new features. Firmware updates
are performed using the PPD (Portable Programming Device).

BEFORE YOU BEGIN
- Firmware updates should only be done when instructed by SALTO support
  or your system administrator.
- Make sure the lock has fresh batteries before updating.
- Updates typically take 30-60 seconds per lock.

STEP-BY-STEP FIRMWARE UPDATE

Check the Current Firmware Version
1. In ProAccess SPACE, navigate to the door you want to update.

2. Open the door's properties or details page.

3. Look for the "Firmware Version" field. Note the current version
   number (for example, 4.12).

4. Compare it with the version you have been asked to install. If it
   already matches, no update is needed.

Download the Firmware to the PPD
5. Connect the PPD to your computer via USB cable.

6. In ProAccess SPACE, go to the PPD management section (under "Tools"
   or "Devices").

7. Select the firmware update file. This may have been provided by
   SALTO support or downloaded from the SALTO partner portal.

8. Click "Download to PPD" or "Transfer." Wait for the download to
   complete.

9. Safely disconnect the PPD from the computer.

Apply the Firmware to the Lock
10. Take the PPD to the lock you need to update.

11. Hold the PPD firmly against the lock's card reader.

12. The PPD will begin transferring the firmware. Keep it steady and
    do not pull it away during the transfer.

13. The lock may flash different colors during the update. This is
    normal. Wait until the PPD screen shows "Complete" or "Success."

14. The process usually takes 30-60 seconds. For some models, it may
    take up to 2 minutes.

Verify After Update
15. Once the PPD confirms success, remove it from the lock.

16. Wait about 10 seconds for the lock to restart with the new firmware.

17. Test the lock with a known working keycard. It should flash green
    and unlock normally.

18. In ProAccess SPACE, check the door's firmware version to confirm
    it now shows the new version number.

IMPORTANT NOTES
- Do not remove the PPD from the lock during the update. This can cause
  the lock to malfunction.
- If the update fails, try again. If it fails a second time, replace
  the batteries and try once more.
- If a lock becomes unresponsive after a failed update, escalate
  immediately to Tier 2 support. Do not attempt further updates.
- Update one lock at a time. Do not rush through multiple locks without
  verifying each one.
