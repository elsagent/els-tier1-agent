SALTO Electronic Lock - Lock Showing Offline Troubleshooting
=============================================================

When a lock shows as "offline" in ProAccess SPACE, the system cannot
communicate with it wirelessly. The lock still works with keycards, but
it will not receive real-time updates such as cancellations or new
access permissions until the connection is restored.

STEP-BY-STEP TROUBLESHOOTING

Check the Lock Battery
1. Low batteries are the most common cause of a lock going offline. A
   lock with weak batteries may not have enough power to maintain its
   wireless connection.

2. Replace the batteries with 4 fresh AA alkaline batteries (see the
   Battery Replacement guide).

3. Wait 2-3 minutes after replacing batteries for the lock to reconnect.

Check the Wireless Connection
4. In ProAccess SPACE, go to the door's properties and check the
   connection status. Look for signal strength or last communication
   timestamp.

5. If the lock was recently installed or moved, it may be out of range
   of the nearest wireless gateway or repeater.

6. Confirm that the wireless gateway nearest to the lock is powered on
   and has its status lights showing normal operation (usually solid
   green).

Update the Lock with a PPD (Portable Programming Device)
7. If the lock cannot connect wirelessly, you can update it manually
   using the PPD.

8. Connect the PPD to ProAccess SPACE via USB and download the latest
   door configuration.

9. Take the PPD to the lock and hold it against the lock's reader.

10. Wait for the PPD to transfer the update. The lock will flash to
    confirm it received the data.

Check the Network
11. Verify that the SALTO server is running and accessible on your
    property's network.

12. Check that the network switch or router that connects the SALTO
    gateways is powered on and functioning.

13. If other locks are also offline, the issue is likely the network or
    server rather than individual locks.

Restart the SALTO Server
14. If many locks are offline or the server appears unresponsive, try
    restarting the SALTO ProAccess SPACE server service.

15. On the server computer, restart the SALTO service from Windows
    Services or restart the computer entirely.

16. Wait 5-10 minutes for all locks to reconnect after a server restart.

WHEN TO ESCALATE
- A single lock stays offline after fresh batteries and PPD update.
- Multiple locks are offline and a server restart did not help.
- The wireless gateway hardware appears damaged or has no power.

Escalate to Tier 2 support with the affected door names and what steps
you have already completed.
