SALTO ProAccess SPACE - Basic Software Navigation Guide
========================================================

ProAccess SPACE is the web-based software used to manage SALTO locks,
keycards, and access permissions. This guide covers the basics of
finding your way around the system.

LOGGING IN

1. Open a web browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).

2. Go to your property's ProAccess SPACE address. This is typically a
   URL like https://your-hotel.salto.ws or a local network address
   provided by your IT team.

3. Enter your username (usually your email address) and password.

4. Click "Login." If you have forgotten your password, see the Password
   Reset guide.

THE DASHBOARD

5. After logging in, you will see the main dashboard. This shows an
   overview of your system, including lock status, recent alerts, and
   quick links.

6. The left-hand menu is your main navigation. The key sections are:
   - Doors (or Access Points)
   - Cardholders (or Users)
   - Monitoring
   - Reports
   - Settings

FINDING DOORS AND ZONES

7. Click "Doors" or "Access Points" in the left menu to see a list of
   all locks in your property.

8. Use the search bar at the top to find a specific room number or
   door name.

9. Click on a door to see its details: status (online/offline), battery
   level, firmware version, and assigned access zones.

10. Zones are groups of doors (for example, "Guest Rooms Floor 3" or
    "Pool Area"). You can find zone settings under "Zones" or within
    the Doors section.

FINDING GUESTS AND STAFF

11. Click "Cardholders" or "Users" in the left menu to see everyone
    with a keycard in the system.

12. Use the search bar to find a person by name or card number.

13. Click on a cardholder to see their details: assigned rooms, valid
    dates, card status, and access history.

REPORTS

14. Click "Reports" in the left menu to access system reports.

15. Common reports include:
    - Door event reports (who accessed which doors).
    - Battery status reports (which locks need new batteries).
    - Card usage reports (active, expired, or cancelled cards).

16. Select the report type, set your date range, and click "Generate."
    Most reports can be exported as CSV or PDF.

SETTINGS

17. Click "Settings" in the left menu for system configuration.

18. As front desk staff, you typically will not need to change settings.
    This area is mainly used by managers and system administrators for
    tasks like adding new locks, creating access zones, or managing
    user accounts.

19. If you need a setting changed, contact your manager or system
    administrator.

TIPS FOR DAILY USE
- Bookmark the ProAccess SPACE login page for quick access.
- Keep the dashboard open during your shift to monitor alerts.
- If the software feels slow or unresponsive, try refreshing the
  browser page. If the problem continues, restart the browser.
- Always log out when you leave the workstation by clicking your name
  or profile icon and selecting "Logout."
