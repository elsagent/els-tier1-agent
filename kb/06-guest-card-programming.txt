SALTO ProAccess SPACE - Guest Card Programming Guide
=====================================================

This guide walks you through creating a guest keycard in ProAccess SPACE.
You will need a blank SALTO-compatible RFID card and an encoder connected
to your computer.

STEP-BY-STEP CARD PROGRAMMING

Open ProAccess SPACE
1. Open ProAccess SPACE in your web browser and log in with your
   username and password.

2. From the main dashboard, click on "Cardholders" or "Users" in the
   left-hand menu (the label depends on your software version).

Create a Guest Cardholder
3. Click the "New" or "Add Cardholder" button.

4. Enter the guest's name in the First Name and Last Name fields.
   Tip: Use the name from the reservation for consistency.

5. Set the cardholder type to "Guest" (if your system uses cardholder
   types).

Assign the Room
6. In the cardholder's access section, click "Add Access" or "Assign
   Access Point."

7. Search for the room number or door name and select it from the list.

8. If the guest also needs access to common areas (pool, gym, parking),
   add those zones as well.

Set Valid Dates and Times
9. Set the "Valid From" date and time to the guest's check-in date and
   your property's standard check-in time (for example, 3:00 PM).

10. Set the "Valid Until" date and time to the guest's check-out date
    and your standard check-out time (for example, 11:00 AM).

11. Double-check these dates. Incorrect dates are the most common cause
    of guest card problems.

Encode the Card
12. Place a blank RFID card on the encoder connected to your computer.

13. Click the "Encode" or "Write Card" button in ProAccess SPACE.

14. Wait for the encoding to complete. You will see a confirmation
    message on screen. The encoder may beep or flash a green light.

15. Remove the card from the encoder.

Test the Card
16. If possible, go to the assigned room and test the card on the lock.
    The lock should flash green and unlock.

17. If you cannot test at the door, at minimum confirm in ProAccess
    SPACE that the card shows as "Active" with the correct room and
    dates.

COMMON ISSUES
- If encoding fails, make sure the encoder is plugged in and recognized
  by the system. Try a different card.
- If the card does not work at the door, verify the dates, room
  assignment, and that the door is online.
- To make a duplicate card for a second guest in the same room, repeat
  the process and assign the same room and dates.
