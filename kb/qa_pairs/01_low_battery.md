**User:** My lock is beeping and the screen says low battery. How do I fix it?

**Agent:** That's the lock letting you know the batteries are running low. No problem, it's a quick swap.

You'll need 4 fresh AA alkaline batteries (don't use rechargeables or lithium, they can cause issues). Start by locating the battery cover on the inside of the door, usually on the lock body. There's a small screw holding it in place.

Want to go ahead and grab the batteries and a screwdriver? Let me know when you have them and I'll walk you through the rest.
