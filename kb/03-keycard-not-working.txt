SALTO Electronic Lock - Keycard Not Working Troubleshooting
============================================================

When a guest or staff member reports their keycard is not opening a door,
work through these steps in order before escalating.

STEP-BY-STEP TROUBLESHOOTING

Try the Card Slowly
1. Ask the person to present the card to the lock slowly and hold it
   steady for 2-3 seconds. Do not tap and pull away quickly.

2. Make sure the card is held flat against the reader, not at an angle.

3. Try presenting the card to a different spot on the reader if the first
   attempt does not work.

Check for Physical Damage
4. Inspect the card for visible damage: cracks, bends, scratches, or
   demagnetization marks.

5. If the card has been near a phone, magnet, or other electronic device,
   it may be damaged. Issue a new card.

6. Check that the card is the correct type for your SALTO system (RFID,
   not magnetic stripe, unless your property uses mag-stripe encoders).

Check Card Expiration
7. Guest cards have check-in and check-out dates programmed on them. If
   the guest's stay has been extended but the card was not updated, it
   will stop working at the original check-out time.

8. Encode a new card with the correct dates if this is the issue.

Try a Different Card
9. Test the lock with a known working staff card or master card. If the
   staff card works, the problem is with the guest's card, not the lock.

10. If no card works on the lock, the issue is likely the lock itself.
    Check the battery (see Battery Replacement guide).

Check Access Permissions
11. Open ProAccess SPACE and look up the guest's cardholder record.

12. Confirm the card is assigned to the correct room and zone.

13. Confirm the valid dates and times are correct.

14. Check that the card has not been cancelled or blocked.

15. If permissions look correct but the card still does not work, try
    encoding a brand new card for the guest.

WHEN TO ESCALATE
- The lock does not respond to any card (no lights, no sound).
- The lock flashes red for every card including staff and master cards.
- You have tried a new card and confirmed permissions but it still fails.

Escalate to Tier 2 support with the door name, card details, and what
you have already tried.
